Technical Services

At Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge, we specialize in technical services that take your hair to the next level. Whether you're after texture, smoothness, or easy styling. If you’re looking to add curls and volume, our Wella perms create long-lasting bounce and definition.

Perm

Perm

At Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge, we offer modern perm services that allow you to embrace beautiful, bouncy curls or waves with a fresh, updated approach. Our expert stylists utilize Wella perms and advanced techniques to ensure your perm enhances your natural beauty while providing long-lasting results.

Whether you're looking for tight, defined curls or soft, beachy waves, we tailor each perm to suit your individual style and hair type.
During your consultation, our stylists will discuss your desired look and recommend the best perm technique to achieve the results you want.

We use gentle Wella formulas to minimize damage and maintain the health of your hair, ensuring a soft, touchable finish that looks and feels fantastic. After your perm, we'll provide you with expert advice on how to care for and style your new curls, so they stay vibrant and bouncy for weeks to come.

Regular maintenance appointments every 6 to 8 weeks for a cut and finish will help keep your perm looking fresh, and our team is here to support you with personalized tips and product recommendations for at-home care.

Chemical Straightening

Chemical Straightening

At Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge, we offer professional chemical straightening services using high-quality Wella products, designed to give you the sleek, straight hair you've always wanted. Chemical straightening works by breaking down the bonds in your hair, allowing us to reshape it into a smooth, glossy finish that lasts.

Our chemical straightening service is perfect for anyone looking to achieve a long-lasting, frizz-free, and straightened style. Whether your hair is curly, wavy, or unruly, this treatment leaves your hair incredibly smooth, shiny, and easy to manage. Unlike temporary methods, chemical straightening provides permanent results, so you can enjoy sleek, straight hair without the daily heat styling.

This service is ideal for those who want:

  • Straight, polished hair that holds its shape through humidity and daily wear.
  • A low-maintenance routine, as your hair will stay straight after each wash.
  • To eliminate frizz and create a sleek, smooth texture.
