Zoe

Senior Stylist

Bio

My journey within the hair industry has been truly fulfilling. I'm in a constant state of learning and doing what I'm passionate about. Adapting to the unique needs of each client is the essence of my hairdressing style. Interacting with diverse individuals daily and contributing to their sense of well-being is incredibly rewarding.

Pinpointing a single highlight or categorizing my career is a tough task, as the entirety of it feels like a series of highlights. The continuous learning journey keeps me engaged; I'm always excited to grasp new techniques rather than mastering just one. My appetite for knowledge spans across all aspects, and I'm enthusiastic about exploring novel avenues.
