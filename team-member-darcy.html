Darcy

Stylist

Bio

I have six years of experience in the hairdressing industry. My work is characterized by precision and a genuine passion for perfection in every haircut and styling detail. Whether it's infusing colour into hair, through highlights, balayage, or even unconventional shades, I truly come alive.

The connection with people is undeniable, and I derive immense satisfaction from the transformation of a blank hair canvas into a vibrant, revitalized form, particularly when working on intricate hair updos. My enthusiasm for hairdressing runs deep. I view the entire industry as something warmly familiar. The ability to inject someone with a renewed sense of self through their hair is a gratifying experience I cherish.

A particular highlight in my journey has been perfecting the art of balayage. Guiding clients from boxed dyes to elegant art of colouring has been incredibly rewarding. The ever-evolving techniques in balayage continue to captivate my interest and push my skills further.
