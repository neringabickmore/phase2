Hair Extensions

Official Beauty Works Partner

Transform your look with premium hair extensions at Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge, where we specialize in delivering natural-looking, seamless results. We proudly use Beauty Works hair extensions, renowned for their high-quality, long-lasting hair that blends flawlessly with your own.

Our expert stylists offer a variety of extension methods to suit your hair type and lifestyle, including tape-in extensions, wefts, and our unique hybrid technique.

Not only can extensions add length and volume, but they’re also an excellent way to change your hair colour without the need for any chemical treatments. Whether you’re looking to add highlights, lowlights, or create a bold new shade, our hair extensions allow you to experiment with different colours while keeping your natural hair untouched.

During your consultation, we’ll help you choose the best method to achieve your desired length, volume, and style. Our extensions are carefully applied to ensure they blend seamlessly with your natural hair, offering both comfort and longevity.

Regular maintenance appointments every 6-8 weeks will keep your extensions looking fresh and healthy, and our team will guide you on how to care for them at home with the right products and techniques.
