Hair Extensions

Official Beauty Works Partner

Transform your look with premium hair extensions at Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge, where we specialize in delivering natural-looking, seamless results. We proudly use Beauty Works hair extensions, renowned for their high-quality, long-lasting hair that blends flawlessly with your own.

Our expert stylists offer a variety of extension methods to suit your hair type and lifestyle, including tape-in extensions, wefts, and our unique hybrid technique.

Not only can extensions add length and volume, but they’re also an excellent way to change your hair colour without the need for any chemical treatments. Whether you’re looking to add highlights, lowlights, or create a bold new shade, our hair extensions allow you to experiment with different colours while keeping your natural hair untouched.

During your consultation, we’ll help you choose the best method to achieve your desired length, volume, and style. Our extensions are carefully applied to ensure they blend seamlessly with your natural hair, offering both comfort and longevity.

Regular maintenance appointments every 6-8 weeks will keep your extensions looking fresh and healthy, and our team will guide you on how to care for them at home with the right products and techniques.

hybrid

hybrid

Our Hybrid Hair Extensions service at Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge blends the benefits of both tape-in and weft extensions to give you a customized, natural, and long-lasting transformation. This innovative approach allows us to create a fuller, more seamless finish while addressing individual styling needs, whether for volume, length, or enhancing specific sections of your hair.

Why Choose the Hybrid Extension Method?

Customizable Volume and Coverage: By combining tapes and wefts, we’re able to strategically place extensions where they’re needed most, adding targeted volume and thickness. We can add tapes for a more natural finish at the crown and hairline, while using wefts for added density through the mid-lengths and ends, creating a balanced, full-bodied look.

Seamless Blending:Tape extensions are thinner and lay flat against the scalp, making them ideal for more visible areas where a discreet blend is essential. Weft extensions provide added coverage and can be customized in density, making them perfect for achieving volume that feels and looks completely natural. Together, they offer an exceptionally seamless integration with your natural hair.

Enhanced Durability: Hybrid extensions offer the best of both worlds: the longevity of wefts and the lightweight, gentle attachment of tapes. This combination can offer an improved wear time, as tapes allow for easy touch-ups while wefts add longer-lasting thickness and resilience.

Versatile Styling: With hybrid extensions, you’ll enjoy the freedom to style your hair in a variety of ways. The tapes allow for discreet blending, which is perfect for updos or pulling hair away from the face, while the added weight of the wefts contributes to a bouncy, voluminous look that holds shape and style.

Less Stress on Natural Hair: By distributing the weight across tapes and wefts, hybrid extensions minimize stress on your natural hair, reducing the risk of strain and maintaining the health and integrity of your scalp and strands. This method is gentle enough for fine or fragile hair, yet durable enough for thicker textures.
