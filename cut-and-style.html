Cut and style

At Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge, we believe a great haircut is the foundation of any style, and our expert stylists are dedicated to crafting looks that are tailored to your unique features and lifestyle. Whether you're after a chic bob, long layers, or a complete restyle, our team will work with you to create a cut that enhances your natural beauty.

The experience starts with a thorough consultation to understand your hair type, texture, and personal preferences. Our stylists are skilled in cutting techniques that bring out the best in your hair, ensuring precision and creativity. From there, we complete your look with a professional blow-dry or style that leaves your hair looking polished and full of movement.

Regular trims are essential for maintaining the health of your hair, preventing split ends, and keeping your style in shape. We recommend coming in every 6-8 weeks to ensure your hair stays fresh, healthy, and vibrant.

Balayage is perfect for those seeking a natural, sun-kissed look with low-maintenance upkeep. It's ideal for clients who want a soft blend of color, especially if you're new to hair colouring. The freehand painting technique allows our expert Phase 2.0 colourists to create a bespoke, graduated effect that enhances your natural tones. Aftercare is simple: to keep your balayage looking fresh, we recommend using a toning shampoo to maintain the vibrancy of your colour. Around six weeks after your initial balayage, a toner session is ideal to refresh your look, followed by another balayage session in around 12 weeks.

If you’re unsure whether to choose balayage or highlights, balayage offers a more natural, subtle finish with less regrowth maintenance. Highlights tend to require more frequent upkeep. Plus, balayage is a fantastic option for those with virgin hair who are looking to ease into colouring—its soft application minimizes damage, making it a gentle introduction.

Reverse balayage is also available for clients looking to darken their hair with the same seamless blend, perfect for transitioning into a deeper tone without losing dimension.

Highlights are a versatile way to add brightness and dimension to your hair, creating a fresh, vibrant look. Perfect for clients who want noticeable contrast and lighter sections throughout their hair, highlights allow for a more defined and structured style compared to balayage. Our expert colourists at Phase 2.0 use precision foil techniques to lift and lighten specific strands, creating depth and enhancing your natural base colour. This technique offers a wide range of customisation, whether you're after bold, high-contrast streaks or subtle, sun-kissed highlights.

To maintain the vibrancy of your highlights, we recommend refreshing them every 6-8 weeks, with a toning session in between to keep brassiness at bay and enhance the brightness. This ensures your colour remains fresh and your hair stays healthy.

If you’re deciding between highlights and balayage, highlights provide a more defined, structured look, especially for those wanting a brighter lift throughout the hair. While balayage has a more blended, natural grow-out, highlights create a crisper contrast and often suit those wanting a sharper, more noticeable lift.
