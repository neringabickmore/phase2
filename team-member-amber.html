Amber

Stylist

Bio

My journey in the hair industry has been a delightful and thrilling adventure. Whether it's crafting voluminous and bouncy styles or achieving a gentle, flowing wave, creating the perfect look for a night out is what I love. Within my collection of skills, I discover that my genuine passion resides in the realm of colour artistry. Transforming hair through bold changes and the finesse of lightening and toning brings me immense joy.

From the outset, I've held a deep-seated passion for the craft of hairdressing. The notion of enhancing people's beauty and boosting their confidence resonates with me. A high point in my career is witnessing the smiles on my clients' faces.

Looking ahead, I foster the aspiration of becoming an expert in blonde shades. The prospect of making blonde shades truly shine and perfecting toning techniques excites me. I'm thrilled to continue learning and striving to bring the best to every client's experience.
