Jodi

Senior Stylist

Bio

My journey into hairdressing commenced in 1999 as an Apprentice at Phase 2. I pride myself on unwavering commitment to intricate detailing, coupled with a sincere and patient approach to tending to clients' needs. Describing my style as a blend of classic and creative elements, I find immense joy in helping clients discover a unique twist that suits them perfectly.

The true satisfaction of my work lies in witnessing clients leave with radiant smiles and a profound sense of happiness. From an early age, my fascination with hair was evident. I would seize any chance to experiment with diverse styles on my mother's hair. This passion only flourished as I delved into my apprenticeship, where the dynamic and sociable salon environment fueled my creativity and enthusiasm.

When reflecting on my journey, it's not a single highlight that shines brightest; rather, I'm immensely proud of the loyal clientele that has grown alongside me throughout the years.
