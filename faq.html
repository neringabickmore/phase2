Our Most Frequently Asked Questions

Balayage is a freehand hair colouring technique that gives a blended, natural look with less noticeable regrowth lines.

Hair toner is a product used primarily to alter the undertones of bleached or dyed hair and to improve the overall look and feel.

A perm, or permanent wave, is a chemical process that alters the internal structure of the hair to create waves or curls. The procedure provides lasting results, typically until the hair grows out or is cut.

The term 'Session Hair' often refers to the hair styling done by professionals for specific events, shoots, or runway shows. These looks are usually highly creative, on-trend, and tailored to meet the needs of the event or project.

Scandi Blonde, short for Scandinavian Blond, refers to a particular shade of blonde hair that is often characterized by its cool, icy, almost platinum tones. It captures the minimalist elegance often associated with Scandinavian design.

A keratin treatment is a chemical procedure that infuses natural keratin protein into the hair cuticle. This treatment aims to smooth frizz, add shine, and simplify the styling process

Maintaining the perfect blonde shade can be challenging due to various factors like sun exposure, chlorine, and even the minerals in your water.

Selecting the appropriate hair care products is crucial for maintaining healthy locks. Seek advise from your hair stylist. We always recommend professional hair care products.

Heat styling is often unavoidable, but that doesn't mean you can't protect your hair.

Curly hair requires specialized care to maintain its natural bounce and minimize frizz.
