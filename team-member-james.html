Bio

personal website

I have over 20 years of expertise in the world of hairstyling. My career spans across working with international sensations and renowned fashion houses, and I am now bringing my passion and creativity to the heart of Canvey Island.

I feel very privileged to have worked with the artists such as Fifth Harmony and Camilla Cabello during their European tours as well as leading the hair team on the illustrious X Factor tour. My hair artistry has graced the pages of top-tier publications like Italian and British Vogue, Dazed, Wonderland, and Harper’s Bazaar. I am very excited and ready to embark on my newest adventure as the proud owner of Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge and Barbers.

I see the salon as a canvas to bring my creative vision to life, fostering growth, and building on the foundation of excellence that already exists. For me, a visit to the salon is more than just a haircut; it's an immersive experience. The moment you step through the doors, you're on a journey of self-discovery and pampering. It's about how you feel from the first greeting to the moment you leave with your stunning new look.

I believe in education as the cornerstone of my salon, ensuring every guest receives unparalleled care and style. I have a unique ability to inspire, and therefore I am ready to take your hair journey to new heights. Having worked in every facet of the industry and collaborated with world-renowned stylists, I'm excited to share my wealth of experience. By nurturing and inspiring the salon's talented professionals, I aim to bring out the very best in them, resulting in a team that's passionate about giving you the look of your dreams.
