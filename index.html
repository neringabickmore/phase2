Our Top Services: Balayage, Highlights, Scandi Blonde, Hair Extensions & More

At Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge, our team of highly trained stylists specializes in a wide range of services, including balayage, creative colour, highlights, custom cuts, and premium hair extensions. Using Beauty Works extensions, we offer tape-ins, wefts, and hybrids to add natural volume, length, and seamless style to your look.

Hair extensions aren’t just for length and volume, they're also a fantastic way to experiment with colour without the commitment. Whether you're looking to add highlights, lowlights, or bold pops of colour, our extensions allow you to transform your look effortlessly while keeping your natural hair protected.

Every appointment begins with a detailed consultation, where we take the time to understand your preferences, lifestyle, and hair goals. Whether you're after a chic bob, a stylish crop, perfectly defined curls, or luscious, fuller hair with extensions, we tailor each service to your unique needs.

Your trust is at the heart of everything we do. Together, we'll embark on a journey of collaborative creativity, rejuvenation, and exceptional care, leaving you feeling confident and refreshed.

Who we are

Why Choose Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge?

At Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge, our commitment goes beyond beautiful hair—we’re passionate about protecting the planet. As a sustainable salon, we’re taking meaningful steps toward a greener future through eco-friendly practices like using microfiber towels, Vish software to reduce colour waste, and sourcing Alfaparf products that align with our values.

Education is at the heart of everything we do. Our stylists continuously refine their skills with advanced training, staying ahead of the latest trends and techniques to bring you cutting-edge styles and services.

But we’re more than just a salon. We’re a space for relaxation and rejuvenation. From the moment you walk in, our goal is to provide a calming experience tailored to your needs, leaving you refreshed, confident, and ready to take on the world.

Join us on this journey of conscious choices, professional growth, and unparalleled care for both you and the environment.
