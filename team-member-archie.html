Archie

Barber

Bio

I'm Archie, and I'm a barber who finds great joy in the hair industry. I love staying up-to-date with the latest trends and bringing them to life for my clients. My barbing style is modern and on-trend, perfectly reflecting what people are looking for today.

What I love most about being a barber is the opportunity to lift people up when they're having a bad day or week. I believe a great haircut can truly boost someone's confidence and make their day. I became a barber to connect with more people and to help them look fresh, clean, and feel great about themselves.

One of the highlights of my career was completing 15 haircuts in a single day, which left me feeling incredibly proud. Moving forward, I want to continue honing my skills so I can provide the best possible experience for each of my clients, regardless of their hair type and texture.
