Honey

New Talent Stylist | Barber

Bio

The salon's warm and welcoming ambiance truly resonates with me. I pride myself on tailoring my services to fit each client's unique preferences. Everyday, I strive to enhance my skills and improve the efficiency of the services I provide. What truly thrills me about this industry is the excitement of facing new challenges daily. There's nothing more rewarding than making a client feel cherished and transforming their appearance, uplifting their day.

My journey in hairdressing began during my school years, where my passion was ignited. The subject not only intrigued but also inspired me to pursue a career as a hairstylist. After successfully completing my NVQ in Hair and Beauty, I was more determined than ever to chase this dream job. Witnessing my growth and progress in this field has only solidified my dedication.

In the future, I aspire to showcase my skills backstage at hair shows. Additionally, I aim to expand the range of services I offer, possibly delving into extensions, or even specializing in a specific technique.
