Keratin Treatment

At Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge, we offer a range of premium keratin treatments using Alfa Parf Liss Design products to transform and smooth your hair. Whether you're looking to reduce frizz, add shine, or achieve a sleek, polished look, our keratin treatments provide long-lasting results while maintaining the health of your hair.
We offer three specialized keratin services tailored to your needs:

  • Lisse Design Precision: This treatment is ideal for clients seeking intense smoothing and sleekness. It delivers long-lasting results, leaving your hair smooth, manageable, and frizz-free for up to 12 weeks.
  • Lamination Treatment: If you're looking for a boost in shine and hydration without altering the natural texture of your hair, the Lamination Treatment adds a protective layer that enhances gloss and reduces frizz, perfect for those who want to maintain volume and movement.
  • Easy Lisse Discipline Treatment: For a lighter, more flexible option, the Easy Lisse Discipline Treatment reduces frizz while keeping your hair soft and natural, offering control and smoothness with a more relaxed finish.

During your consultation, we’ll assess your hair type and goals to recommend the best keratin treatment for you. All of our treatments are designed to leave your hair feeling silky, shiny, and easier to manage. Results typically last 6-12 weeks, depending on the treatment and your hair’s condition.

Lisse Precision Treatment

The Alfaparf Lisse Design Precision Treatment offers the ultimate in sleek, straight hair with no compromise on health. This formaldehyde-free keratin smoothing treatment transforms even the most resistant hair, delivering lasting smoothness and shine for up to 4 months. It's designed to eliminate frizz, reduce styling time, and strengthen the hair's structure, making it the perfect option for clients wanting a long-lasting, low-maintenance style. The treatment can be customized for a variety of finishes, from straight and sleek to naturally smooth.

The Luxury of the smoothness you've always wanted. It is dedicated to the women with curly or wavey hair who want it to be strictly and perfectly smooth.

Lamination Treatment

Our Alfaparf Lisse Lamination Treatment enhances your hair’s shine and vitality, creating a smooth, glossy finish that mimics the look of perfectly healthy, polished hair. This lamination process works by coating the hair shaft, helping to seal in moisture while protecting it from external damage. It leaves the hair incredibly soft, frizz-free, and full of reflective shine, with results lasting up to 6 weeks. Ideal for anyone looking to add extra gloss and smoothness without compromising hair’s natural movement.

The Luxury of perfect, shiny hair, instantly, everyday. For the women who have dull, brittle hair, who immediately want a result with extreme shine and vitality.
