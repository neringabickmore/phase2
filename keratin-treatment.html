Keratin Treatment

At Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge, we offer a range of premium formaldehyde-free keratin treatments using Alfa Parf Lisse Design products to transform and smooth your hair. Whether you're looking to reduce frizz, add shine, or achieve a sleek, polished look, our keratin treatments provide long-lasting results while maintaining the health of your hair.
We offer three specialized keratin services tailored to your needs:

  • Lisse Design Precision: This treatment is ideal for clients seeking intense smoothing and sleekness. It delivers long-lasting results, leaving your hair smooth, manageable, and frizz-free for up to 12 weeks.
  • Lisse Lamination Treatment: If you're looking for a boost in shine and hydration without altering the natural texture of your hair, the Lamination Treatment adds a protective layer that enhances gloss and reduces frizz, perfect for those who want to maintain volume and movement.
  • Easy Lisse Discipline Treatment: For a lighter, more flexible option, the Easy Lisse Discipline Treatment reduces frizz while keeping your hair soft and natural, offering control and smoothness with a more relaxed finish.

During your consultation, we’ll assess your hair type and goals to recommend the best keratin treatment for you. All of our treatments are designed to leave your hair feeling silky, shiny, and easier to manage. Results typically last 6-12 weeks, depending on the treatment and your hair’s condition.

Lisse Design Precision

Lisse Design Precision

Our Phase 2.0 stylists specialize in the Alfaparf Lisse Design Precision Treatment, offering the ultimate in sleek, straight hair with no compromise on health. This formaldehyde-free keratin smoothing treatment transforms even the most resistant hair, delivering lasting smoothness and shine for up to 4 months.
Designed to eliminate frizz, reduce styling time, and strengthen the hair’s structure, this treatment is ideal for clients seeking a long-lasting, low-maintenance style. It is the luxury of the smoothness you’ve always wanted.
This treatment is dedicated to women with curly or wavy hair who desire strictly and perfectly smooth hair. Our expert stylists at Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge customize the treatment to create your desired finish, whether straight and sleek or naturally smooth, while leaving your hair strong, shiny, and easy to manage.

Lisse Lamination

Our Phase 2.0 stylists offer the Alfaparf Lisse Lamination Treatment, designed to give you the luxury of perfectly shiny hair every day with little effort.
This innovative treatment creates a sleek, high-gloss finish by coating each strand to lock in moisture and protect your hair from external damage. This treatment is dedicated to women with dull or brittle hair who immediately want a result with extreme shine and vitality. The lamination treatment leaves your hair soft, smooth, and radiant with mirror-like shine for up to 6 weeks. Experience a polished, healthy look tailored by our expert Phase 2.0 stylists to maintain your hair’s natural movement while enhancing its smoothness.
