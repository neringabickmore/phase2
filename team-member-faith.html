Faith

Barber

Bio

The journey through the hair industry has truly shaped my present self. Prior to embarking on the path of a stylist/barber, I suffered with intense anxiety, struggling in basic conversations and lacking in confidence. However, this profession acted as a catalyst, drawing me out of my comfort zone and instilling the much-needed self-assurance to not only feel good about myself but to also uplift others.

My approach to hairstyling is incredibly versatile, adapting to each client's unique preferences. What brings me the greatest satisfaction is the ability to deliver impeccable outcomes to my clients, gaining their recognition for the dedicated effort I put in, and witnessing them depart with smiles of contentment.

Looking ahead I aim to master colouring techniques like balayage, root melt, ombres, and more, to offer clients diverse hair transformation options through enhanced skills.
