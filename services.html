Salon policies

All color services must be followed up with a post-color blow-dry or a cut & finish.

Color services are very complex, and we are only able to provide a starting price, displayed above. Your stylist will advise about the final service price at the consultation stage.

Once you are fully aware of what’s involved and happy with the quote, only then your stylist will proceed with the service.

Please note, color prices do not include a blow-dry. All color services require either a blow-dry or a cut & finish. Toner is charged in addition to your color service.

Consultation

If you are new to the salon or would like a change, why not come in for a free consultation before your appointment?

Patch Test

Your well-being is essential to us and part of that is preventing allergic reactions from colour. We, therefore, require new guests as well as the guests who haven’t seen us for six months to have a patch test 48hrs before any technical service.

Cancellation

Sometimes life gets in your way, and you must cancel your appointment. We get it. Please give us as much notice as possible.
Our cancellation policy is as follows:
48 hours no charge; 24 hours 50% charge, no show 100% charge.

Payment

We accept cash as well as all major credit & debit cards.
