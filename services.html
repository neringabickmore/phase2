Our services

Our wide range of services is crafted to meet every client's hair needs. From expert colour work to precision cutting and bespoke hair extensions, we pride ourselves on delivering tailored results. Each service is backed by our team's dedication to ongoing training and mastery in their craft. Let's explore what we offer.

Colouring

Hair colouring is a transformative way to refresh your look, enhance your natural shade, or make a bold change. At Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge, we offer expert techniques like balayage and highlights to create bespoke colours that suit your style. Whether you want to cover greys, add dimension, or try something new, our team ensures beautiful, lasting results.

Read more

Balayage

Balayage is a freehand highlighting technique that creates a natural, sun-kissed look. Known also as 'sunkissed highlights' or 'ombre', balayage is perfect for those wanting dimension without the maintenance of traditional highlights.
Read more

Highlights

Add brightness and dimension to your hair with our highlighting services. Whether you're after 'foils', 'face-framing highlights', or a 'partial highlight', we tailor the service to your desired look.
Read more

Scandi Blonde

Achieve a stunning, icy look with our Scandi blonde service, also referred to as 'ice-blond', 'full head scalp bleach' or simply 'scalp bleach'. This technique is perfect for creating that striking, luminous finish.
Read more

Toning

After your colour service, we recommend a toner to refine and enhance your colour. This service is also known as 'colour balancing' or 'colour correcting', ensuring your hair looks vibrant and fresh while adding the luxurious and chic finish.
Read more

Colour correction

Phase 2.0 Colour Correction (A.K.A. Colour Change) service is perfect for when your hair isn't quite what you hoped for, or when you're ready for a complete transformation. Whether you're fixing uneven tones, unwanted brassiness or fading colour, we'll restore balance and bring your hair back to its best.
Read more

Cut & style

Our Cut and Style service is all about crafting a look that works for you. Whether you're after a fresh trim, a complete restyle, a little shape-up or just styling, we'll shape your hair to suit your personality, lifestyle and the occasion. Whatever your vibe, we'll work with you to create a look that feels effortless and stylish.

Read more

Session Hair

Session Hair service is perfect for those special occasions when you need a flawless, long-lasting look. Whether you're preparing for a wedding, a photo shoot or a night out, we'll create a stunning style that enhances your natural beauty. From elegant hair-ups to intricate braids and glamorous waves, we specialize in creating show-stopping event styles that stay put all day or night. Whatever your occasion, we'll ensure your hair is styled to perfection, leaving you feeling confident and ready to shine.
Read more

Hair Extensions

We offer a variety of extension types, including 'tape-ins', 'wefts', and 'hybrid extensions'. Extensions can also be used to change your hair colour without applying colour to your own hair.

Read more

Keratin Treatment

Transform your hair with our keratin treatments, which may be referred to as 'brazilian blowdry' 'smoothing treatments' or 'anti-frizz treatments'. These services help to manage frizz and create silky, smooth strands.
Read more

Technical Services

At Phase 2.0 Hair Lounge we offer perm and chemical straightening services:
Experience sleek, straight hair with our chemical straightening services. Sometimes called 'permanent straightening' or 'smoothing treatments', this service eliminates frizz and keeps your hair looking polished.
Embrace beautiful curls with our modern perm services, also known as 'body waves'. Our stylists tailor each service to achieve the perfect curl pattern for your desired look.

Hair treatments

Revitalize your locks with our hair treatments, which may be referred to as 'deep conditioning treatments', 'restorative treatments', or even 'hair masks'. We use a variety of Alfaparf products tailored to your hair needs.
