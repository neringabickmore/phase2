Lily-Mae

Apprentice

I'm Lily-Mae, and I'm pursuing a career as a hairstylist because hairdressing is both rewarding and creatively fulfilling. I love that this field lets me work with a wide variety of clients, create beautiful styles, and make a positive impact on people's lives. From the joy of being hands-on creative to constantly learning and growing, there are so many reasons I'm passionate about becoming a stylist.

In the next five years, I see myself advancing to a senior stylist role, working with top celebrity clients and bringing my skills to the next level. I can't wait to continue this journey and build meaningful connections with each client along the way.
