Daisy

New Talent

Bio

My journey in the hair industry has been truly rewarding. Witnessing the diverse techniques and creative skills of everyone around me has been enlightening. Currently, my focus is on taking my time to master and personalize haircuts to meet my clients' expectations, while steadily building my clientele. The joy of hairdressing for me lies in the happiness and confidence I bring to my clients.

Since childhood, I've dreamt of becoming a hairdresser. So, upon leaving school, I wholeheartedly pursued this passion. A recent highlight of my career is successfully qualifying as a professional, and I'm eagerly looking forward to the opportunities the future holds. I'm also keen on enrolling in additional courses to expand the range of services I can offer to clients.
