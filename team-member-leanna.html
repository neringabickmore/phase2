Leanna

Senior Stylist

Bio

I've been a part of the Phase 2 team since joining for work experience at the age of 15. After qualifying my journey with Phase 2 continued, allowing me to nurture a wonderful clientele. Over the years, I've completed multiple Wella colour courses in London. These experiences have equipped me with a diverse range of colouring techniques that I apply to enhance hairdressing.

My approach to hairstyling is flexible, adapting to the unique preferences and needs of each client. I prioritize detailed consultations. This helps me understand not only the desired outcome but also the feasibility of the style.

One of the most rewarding aspects of my job is the personal connections I build with my clients. Each individual has a distinct story. I cherish the opportunity to contribute to their happiness. The pinnacle of my career is the thriving clientele I've developed. It's a testament to the value of my training and dedication.
